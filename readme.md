## Pong 2:  bolado pra dedel

## Overview

Welcome to Pong with Powers, a thrilling twist on the classic Pong game, developed for the GamedevJS competition using the experimental version of the Gamasource engine. This game takes the timeless gameplay of Pong and infuses it with exciting power-ups and abilities, adding a whole new dimension to the arcade classic.

---

## Features

- **Classic Gameplay**: Enjoy the familiar Pong experience with a modern twist.
- **Power-ups Galore**: Experience an array of power-ups that can turn the tide of the game in an instant.
- **Responsive Controls**: Smooth and intuitive controls ensure a seamless gaming experience.
- **Dynamic AI**: Face off against challenging AI opponents that adapt to your skills.
- **Experimental Gamasource Engine**: Developed using the cutting-edge experimental version of the Gamasource engine, pushing the boundaries of game development.

---

## How to Play

1. **Controls**:
   - **Player 1**: Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your paddle.
   - **Player 2**: Utilize arrow keys to maneuver your paddle.
2. **Gameplay**:
   - Deflect the ball using your paddle to prevent it from reaching your side of the screen.
   - Score points by getting the ball past your opponent's paddle.
   - Watch out for power-ups that appear during gameplay. They can give you an edge or hinder your opponent

---

## Development

This game was developed as an entry for the GamedevJS competition using the experimental version of the Gamasource engine. The focus during development was to create an engaging and dynamic gameplay experience while exploring the capabilities of the Gamasource engine.
